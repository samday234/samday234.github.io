ABOUT ME

Hey There! My name is Samantha Day. I’m a lover of nature, food, traveling and art. I’m a graphic design major at Massart. I spend a lot of time in a dorm full of easy mac and scrap paper working hard to make the best work I can for my classes. So far my time here has been pretty great ans I can’t wait to start doing internships and start working in the field. I’m not completely settled on exactly what I want to do yet, but I aspire to experience working in both smaller businesses as well as larger firms.
